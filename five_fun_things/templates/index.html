Five Fun Things was inspired by article in Private Eye. The premise was rather than worry about eating 5 fruit and veg a day, its better to do five fun things a day. Plug for Private Eye

This is a easy way to journal five fun things for good mental health every day. With helpful reminders to make this a habit. I nice view of list of you past fun things and word cloud of your previous fun things. See for example screenshots.

Menu

  • Screenshots (User logged in)
  • Other Apps and resources
  • Enter some fun things
  • List of your fun things
  • Word cloud of your fun things
  • Account login/out

Mobile App - Easy Install

  • Make sure this webpage is open on mobile device in Safari or Chrome
  • From the Safarie/Chrome menu
  • Choose 'Add to Home Screen'
