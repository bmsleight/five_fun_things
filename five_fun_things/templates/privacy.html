Five Fun Things only discloses logged in user and commenter IP addresses under the same circumstances that it uses and discloses personally-identifying information as described below.

Cookies

Five Fun Things uses "Cookies" to display personalized content.

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor's computer, and that the visitor's browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Five Fun Things uses cookies to help Five Fun Things identify and track visitors, their usage of https://FiveFunThings.com, and their website access preferences. Five Fun Things visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using Five Fun Things's websites, with the drawback that certain features of Five Fun Things's websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

By continuing to navigate our website without changing your cookie settings, you hereby acknowledge and agree to Five Fun Things's use of cookies.
